How It All Started

Maison started in 2016 as a small furniture workshop in New York City. Our founder, driven by a love for craftsmanship and design, wanted to create furniture that was both beautiful and built to last.

Today, we work with over 50 designers across the world to bring you a curated collection of furniture and home décor that transforms any space into a sanctuary.
